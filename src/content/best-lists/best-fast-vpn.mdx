## Testing Methodology

All speed tests were conducted from a 1 Gbps fiber connection in Dallas, Texas. We tested each VPN's WireGuard (or equivalent modern protocol) implementation, connecting to the nearest available server. Each result represents the average of five tests taken at different times of day.
