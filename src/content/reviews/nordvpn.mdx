NordVPN has been a dominant force in the VPN market for over a decade. With Nord Security's acquisition of Surfshark in 2022 and continued investment in infrastructure, NordVPN shows no signs of slowing down.

## Speed & Performance

NordVPN's custom NordLynx protocol, built on WireGuard, delivered impressive results in our tests. On a 1 Gbps fiber connection, we measured average download speeds of 890 Mbps with NordLynx — losing only about 11% of baseline throughput. OpenVPN UDP was slower at 520 Mbps but still more than adequate for 4K streaming.

Latency increased by an average of 35 ms on nearby servers and 150-200 ms on intercontinental connections. Gaming remains playable on nearby servers but competitive gamers should choose servers close to their physical location.

## Streaming Unblocking

NordVPN reliably unblocked Netflix (US, UK, Japan), Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, and HBO Max throughout our testing period. BBC iPlayer access was consistent from US servers. Hulu proved slightly more challenging, occasionally requiring server switching.

## Privacy & Security

NordVPN's no-logs policy has been independently audited three times by PwC and once by Deloitte. The company's RAM-only server infrastructure ensures no data persists after reboot — a critical privacy feature.

## Verdict

NordVPN earns our Editors' Choice award for best all-around VPN. The combination of speed, server coverage, security features, and proven privacy practices is unmatched in the industry. While the renewal pricing stings, the value during initial terms is excellent.
