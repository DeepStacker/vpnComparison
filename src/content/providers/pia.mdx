Private Internet Access has been operating since 2010 and is one of the longest-running consumer VPN services. Based in the United States, the company operates RAM-only servers across over 10,000 IPs in 84 countries. While the US is a Five Eyes member, PIA's proven no-logs policy has held up in court — the company has demonstrated it cannot provide user data in response to legal requests.

PIA supports WireGuard, OpenVPN, and IKEv2 protocols. The service includes MACE, a DNS-level ad, tracker, and malware blocker. Port forwarding is available on all servers, making PIA a top choice for torrenting. The service offers unlimited bandwidth and up to 10 simultaneous connections.

Pricing is competitive, with the three-year plan bringing the effective monthly cost under $2. The service accepts credit cards, PayPal, Bitcoin, and gift cards.
