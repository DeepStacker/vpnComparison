## What Makes a VPN Private?

Privacy isn't just about encryption strength — it's about the entire system: jurisdiction (does the law force data retention?), policy (what data is collected?), audits (is the policy verified?), infrastructure (can data be seized?), and payment (can you pay anonymously?).

## Why Mullvad Leads

Mullvad accepts cash payments sent through the mail — no email, no name, no address. The company doesn't even have your email address after signup. Combined with a verified no-logs policy, extensive audits, and transparent infrastructure, Mullvad offers the highest privacy standard available.
