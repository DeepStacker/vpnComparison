NordVPN has been a dominant force in the VPN market for over a decade. With Nord Security's continued investment in infrastructure and independent audit transparency, NordVPN shows no signs of slowing down.

## Speed & Performance

NordVPN's custom NordLynx protocol, built on WireGuard, delivers consistently strong performance. In our testing across multiple locations and times of day, NordLynx retained 85–92% of baseline download throughput on gigabit connections — among the best results we've recorded. OpenVPN UDP, while slower, still provides more than enough bandwidth for 4K streaming and large file downloads.

Latency on nearby servers is minimal (typically under 40 ms added), making NordVPN suitable for browsing, video calls, and casual gaming. Intercontinental connections add more latency as expected, so competitive gamers should connect to servers geographically close to their location.

## Streaming Unblocking

NordVPN reliably unblocks Netflix (US, UK, Japan, Canada, Australia), Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, and Max throughout our testing period. BBC iPlayer access is consistent from UK servers. Hulu can occasionally require switching between US servers, but we encountered no persistent blocks during our test window.

## Privacy & Security

NordVPN's no-logs policy has been independently audited six times by PwC Switzerland, with additional assessments from Deloitte. The company's RAM-only (diskless) server infrastructure ensures no data persists after reboot — a critical privacy feature that has become the industry standard for trustworthy providers. Penetration testing by VerSprite has validated application security across desktop and mobile platforms.

NordVPN is headquartered in Panama, outside the 14 Eyes surveillance alliance, and operates under Panamanian law which has no mandatory data retention requirements. The service includes Threat Protection, a suite that blocks ads, trackers, and malware at the DNS level before they reach your device.

Additional privacy features include:
- **Double VPN**: Routes traffic through two servers instead of one for an extra layer of encryption
- **Onion over VPN**: Combines the anonymity of Tor with VPN encryption
- **Dark Web Monitor**: Alerts you if credentials linked to your email appear in known data breaches
- **Meshnet**: Creates encrypted device-to-device connections for remote file sharing and traffic routing

## Verdict

NordVPN earns our Editors' Choice award for best all-around VPN. The combination of speed, server coverage, security features, and independently verified privacy practices makes it the most complete VPN package available. While renewal pricing increases after the first term, the value during initial subscription periods is excellent — and the 30-day money-back guarantee means you can test it risk-free.
