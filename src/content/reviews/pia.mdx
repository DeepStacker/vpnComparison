Private Internet Access has been a trusted name in the VPN space since 2010. Acquired by Kape Technologies in 2019, PIA maintains operational independence and continues to publish transparency reports and audit results.

## Performance

PIA's WireGuard implementation delivers solid speeds. We measured 420 Mbps average on a 500 Mbps connection — respectable if not class-leading. OpenVPN was slower at 280 Mbps. Connection times are under 3 seconds with WireGuard.

## Privacy & Security

PIA's no-logs policy has been independently audited by Deloitte. The company operates RAM-only servers and publishes quarterly transparency reports. As a US-based provider, PIA falls under Five Eyes jurisdiction, but its no-logs track record and court-proven resistance to data requests mitigate this concern.

## Verdict

PIA is an excellent choice for torrenting and privacy-focused users on a budget. The port forwarding feature alone makes it the best VPN for P2P users. But if streaming is your priority, look elsewhere.
