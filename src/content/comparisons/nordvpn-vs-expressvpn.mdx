## Speed & Performance

ExpressVPN leads on raw speed thanks to Lightway, but NordVPN's NordLynx is a close second. In real-world use, the difference is negligible for most activities. NordVPN's larger server network means more nearby server options for lower latency.

## Privacy & Jurisdiction

NordVPN's Panama jurisdiction offers stronger privacy protections than ExpressVPN's British Virgin Islands base — both are outside 14 Eyes, but Panama lacks the UK-US intelligence sharing agreements that impact the BVI. Both services have independently audited no-logs policies and RAM-only server infrastructure.

## Streaming

Both services reliably unblock all major streaming platforms. ExpressVPN has a slight edge with Hulu and HBO Max consistency, while NordVPN offers more global Netflix regions.

## Features

NordVPN wins decisively on features — Double VPN, Onion over VPN, Threat Protection ad blocking, dedicated IP, and split tunneling. ExpressVPN is more minimalist, focusing on core VPN functionality.

## Pricing

NordVPN's two-year plan at $3.49/month undercuts ExpressVPN's $4.16/month. Both offer 30-day money-back guarantees.

## Verdict

NordVPN wins for most users thanks to better value and a richer feature set. Choose ExpressVPN if you prioritize the absolute fastest connection speeds or need MediaStreamer DNS for non-VPN devices.
