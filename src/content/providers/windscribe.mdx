Windscribe was founded in 2015 and has grown through a focus on innovation and user choice. Based in Canada — a Five Eyes member — the company's no-logs policy provides the primary privacy protection. Windscribe has published transparency reports and warrant canaries on its website.

The service operates 500+ servers across 63 countries. While the network is smaller than category leaders, Windscribe compensates with unique features. R.O.B.E.R.T. allows users to create custom DNS block lists covering ads, trackers, malware, and specific website categories. The Build-a-Plan pricing lets subscribers pay only for the server locations they want to use.

Windscribe supports WireGuard, OpenVPN, and IKEv2. The paid plan includes unlimited simultaneous connections and port forwarding. The free plan offers 10 GB of monthly data across servers in 10+ countries.
