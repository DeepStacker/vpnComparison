## Step 1: Choose a VPN Provider

Select a VPN from our curated best lists. Consider what matters most: speed, streaming, privacy, or budget. Sign up for their service — most offer 30-day money-back guarantees.

## Step 2: Download and Install

Download the official app from the VPN provider's website (not an app store, to avoid fake apps). Install it like any other application on your device.

## Step 3: Log In and Connect

Log in with the account credentials you created during signup. Most VPNs offer a "Quick Connect" button that automatically selects the best server for your location.

## Step 4: Verify Your VPN is Working

Visit a website like whatismyip.com before and after connecting. Your IP address should change to one belonging to your VPN provider. Check for DNS and WebRTC leaks using online leak testing tools.

## Step 5: Configure for Your Needs

- **For privacy:** Connect to a server in a privacy-friendly jurisdiction.
- **For streaming:** Connect to a server in the country whose content you want to access.
- **For torrenting:** Connect to a P2P-optimized server if your provider offers them.

## Troubleshooting

- **Can't connect?** Try switching protocols (WireGuard to OpenVPN or vice versa).
- **Slow speeds?** Try a server closer to your physical location.
- **Streaming blocked?** Try a different server in the same country.
