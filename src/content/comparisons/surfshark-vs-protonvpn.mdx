## Speed & Performance

Surfshark delivers faster speeds than Proton VPN across all protocols. WireGuard on Surfshark averaged 620 Mbps vs Proton VPN's 480 Mbps on our 1 Gbps test connections. Both services add 35-50 ms latency on nearby servers.

## Streaming

Surfshark reliably unblocks Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, and BBC iPlayer. Proton VPN only reliably unblocks Amazon Prime Video and BBC iPlayer — Netflix is inconsistent and most other platforms are blocked.

## Features

Surfshark offers unlimited simultaneous connections, CleanWeb ad blocking, MultiHop, and GPS spoofing. Proton VPN counters with NetShield ad blocking, Secure Core, and a genuine free tier.

## Privacy

Proton VPN's Swiss jurisdiction offers stronger privacy protections than Surfshark's Netherlands base. Both have independently audited no-logs policies. Proton VPN's open-source apps and transparency reports give it an edge.

## Pricing

Surfshark's $2.49/month two-year plan is cheaper than Proton VPN's $4.49/month two-year plan. But Proton VPN's free tier offers a legitimate no-cost option.

## Verdict

Surfshark wins for most users with better speeds, streaming, and value. Proton VPN is the better choice if you need a free VPN or prioritize Swiss privacy jurisdiction.
