## Privacy & Logging

PIA is based in the US (Five Eyes) while CyberGhost operates from Romania (outside 14 Eyes). Romania has the jurisdictional advantage. Both have independently audited no-logs policies and use RAM-only servers.

**Winner:** CyberGhost — Romania jurisdiction is significantly stronger for privacy than the US.

## Torrenting

This is where PIA separates from the pack. PIA offers port forwarding on every server — the best P2P implementation in any consumer VPN. CyberGhost has no port forwarding at all.

**Winner:** PIA — port forwarding is a game-changer for torrenting.

## Streaming

CyberGhost's dedicated streaming profiles make it the clear winner for streaming. One-click access to Netflix, BBC iPlayer, Disney+, and more. PIA can unblock some services but requires server switching.

**Winner:** CyberGhost — dedicated streaming servers eliminate the guesswork.

## Pricing & Value

PIA's three-year plan at $1.94/month undercuts CyberGhost's $2.37/month on the two-year plan. Both offer strong value.

**Winner:** PIA — slightly cheaper with port forwarding included.

## Verdict

PIA wins for torrenting users with its port forwarding and lower price. CyberGhost wins for streaming-focused users with its dedicated profiles and better jurisdiction. Choose based on your primary use case.
