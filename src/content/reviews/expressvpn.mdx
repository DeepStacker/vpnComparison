ExpressVPN has been a premium player since 2009, building a reputation for reliability that few competitors match. Its proprietary Lightway protocol and TrustedServer technology represent genuine innovation in the VPN space.

## Speed & Performance
ExpressVPN's Lightway protocol is exceptionally fast. We recorded average download speeds of 920 Mbps on a 1 Gbps connection — the highest we've seen from any VPN. Connection times are nearly instant at under 2 seconds. OpenVPN UDP delivered 580 Mbps, still competitive with most VPNs' WireGuard implementations.

## Streaming
ExpressVPN is the most reliable streaming VPN we've tested. Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, HBO Max, Hulu, and BBC iPlayer all worked without interruption throughout testing. The included MediaStreamer DNS is a genuine differentiator for users with game consoles, smart TVs, and Apple TV.

## Privacy
ExpressVPN's TrustedServer technology ensures all servers run on RAM, with no data written to hard drives. The company's no-logs policy has been verified by PwC, and the Lightway protocol underwent independent security review by Cure53.
