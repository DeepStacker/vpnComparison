Mullvad is not trying to be everything to everyone. It's a VPN for people who prioritize privacy above all else. The service strips away everything that doesn't serve that mission, resulting in a focused, principled product.

## Speed & Performance
Mullvad's WireGuard implementation is excellent — we measured 850 Mbps average download speeds on a 1 Gbps connection. OpenVPN UDP averaged 510 Mbps. With fewer users per server and high-bandwidth infrastructure, Mullvad often feels faster than services with larger networks. Connection times with WireGuard are under 2 seconds.

## Streaming
Mullvad does not unblock streaming services. This is by design — the company explicitly states it is a privacy tool, not a streaming unblocker. BBC iPlayer worked in our tests, but Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, and Hulu were all blocked.

## Privacy & Anonymity
Mullvad takes privacy further than any competitor. You can pay with cash sent through the postal service — no email, no name, no address required. The no-logs policy has been verified by Assured AB in a comprehensive audit. All apps and infrastructure undergo regular third-party penetration testing.

## Verdict
Mullvad is the right choice if your priority is privacy. If you need streaming unblocking, advanced features, or a massive server network, look elsewhere.
