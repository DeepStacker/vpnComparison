question: "Can VPNs be blocked by governments or websites?"
answer: "Yes. Countries like China, Russia, Iran, and the UAE employ deep packet inspection (DPI) to detect and block VPN traffic. Some VPNs use obfuscation techniques to disguise traffic as regular HTTPS. Streaming services also block known VPN IP addresses. Premium VPNs with large IP pools and obfuscation features are better equipped to evade blocking."
