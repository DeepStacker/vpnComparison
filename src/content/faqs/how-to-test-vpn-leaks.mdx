question: "How do I test if my VPN is leaking my IP address or DNS?"
answer: "Connect to your VPN, then visit ipleak.net or dnsleaktest.com. These sites will show the IP address and DNS servers your browser is actually using. If you see your real IP address or your ISP's DNS servers, your VPN is leaking. Most reputable VPNs have built-in leak protection, but it's good practice to verify after connection. Check for DNS, IPv6, and WebRTC leaks specifically."
