Founded in 2011, CyberGhost has grown into one of the world's most popular consumer VPNs. Based in Bucharest, Romania, the company operates under some of Europe's strongest privacy protections. Romania is outside the 14 Eyes surveillance alliance, giving CyberGhost a jurisdictional advantage.

The service operates 11,000+ servers across 100 countries. CyberGhost owns its physical server infrastructure, which provides greater control over security and performance. All servers run on RAM-only architecture. The network includes dedicated streaming servers optimized for specific platforms like Netflix, BBC iPlayer, and Disney+.

CyberGhost offers a 45-day money-back guarantee on long-term plans — the longest in the industry. The service supports up to 7 simultaneous connections and provides apps for Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS, Android TV, and browser extensions.
