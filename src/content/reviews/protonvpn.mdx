Proton VPN enters the market with a compelling proposition — privacy-first design from the creators of Proton Mail, backed by Swiss law. The service earns high marks for transparency and genuine commitment to user privacy.

## Speed & Performance
Proton VPN's WireGuard implementation averaged 480 Mbps on our 500 Mbps test connection — acceptable but noticeably slower than NordVPN and ExpressVPN. OpenVPN UDP averaged 310 Mbps. Additional latency averaged 50 ms on nearby servers and up to 220 ms on intercontinental connections. These speeds are adequate for streaming and browsing but noticeable in gaming.

## Streaming
Proton VPN is not a streaming-first VPN. Netflix US access was inconsistent, Disney+ only worked occasionally, and HBO Max was entirely blocked. Amazon Prime Video and BBC iPlayer worked reliably. For users who prioritize streaming, other services are better options.

## The Free Tier
Unlike fake free tiers that throttle to unusable speeds or impose strict data caps, Proton VPN's free plan is genuinely functional — unlimited data, no logs, and servers in three countries. The 2 Mbps speed cap is enough for standard definition streaming and browsing.
