CyberGhost has established itself as one of the most user-friendly VPNs on the market. Headquartered in Romania — a jurisdiction with strong privacy protections outside the 14 Eyes alliance — the company has invested heavily in making VPNs accessible to non-technical users.

## Streaming Performance

CyberGhost's dedicated streaming servers are a genuine differentiator. Instead of guessing which server works for Netflix, you simply pick "Netflix" from the streaming profile list and connect. During testing, this approach delivered consistent access to Netflix US (including library browsing), Disney+, Amazon Prime Video, HBO Max, and BBC iPlayer.

## Privacy & Security

Romania's privacy-friendly jurisdiction gives CyberGhost a genuine advantage over US-based competitors. The company's no-logs policy has been audited by Deloitte. All servers use RAM-only infrastructure.

## Verdict

CyberGhost is an excellent choice for anyone who wants a VPN that just works for streaming. The dedicated profiles remove the guesswork, and the Romania jurisdiction provides strong privacy protections. Speeds are adequate but not the fastest.
