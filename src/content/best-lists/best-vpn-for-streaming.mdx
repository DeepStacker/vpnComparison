## How We Tested

We evaluated each VPN's ability to unblock Netflix (US, UK, and Japan libraries), Amazon Prime Video, Disney+, HBO Max, Hulu, and BBC iPlayer. Each service was tested from three US locations and one UK location over a 14-day period. A streaming VPN needed to maintain access throughout the testing window without requiring server switching.

## Why ExpressVPN Wins

ExpressVPN's combination of Lightway protocol speed and MediaStreamer DNS makes it the most versatile streaming VPN. The DNS-based approach works on game consoles, smart TVs, and Apple TV — devices that don't support traditional VPN apps.

## Why Not Just Use a Free VPN?

Free VPNs rarely work with streaming services, and those that do often impose strict data caps that make movie watching impractical. More importantly, free VPNs often log your activity or sell bandwidth — a poor trade-off for saving a few dollars.
