Surfshark launched in 2018 and has rapidly grown into one of the most popular VPN services. Its unlimited device policy and aggressive pricing make it attractive for budget-conscious users and large households.

## Speed & Performance
Surfshark's WireGuard implementation delivered average speeds of 620 Mbps on our 1 Gbps test connection. While significantly slower than NordVPN's NordLynx or ExpressVPN's Lightway, this is still more than sufficient for 4K streaming, gaming, and large downloads. OpenVPN UDP averaged 380 Mbps. Connection times are solid at 3-5 seconds with WireGuard.

## Streaming
Surfshark reliably unblocked Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, and Disney+ across multiple regions. BBC iPlayer access was consistent from US and European servers. Hulu and HBO Max were less reliable, occasionally requiring server switching to find a working connection.

## Value Proposition
At $2.49/month on the two-year plan, Surfshark is exceptional value — especially considering the feature set includes CleanWeb ad blocking, MultiHop, and unlimited device connections.
