Surfshark launched in 2018 and has rapidly grown into one of the most popular VPN services. Its unlimited device policy and aggressive pricing make it attractive for budget-conscious users and large households.

## Speed & Performance
Surfshark's WireGuard implementation delivers solid throughput — more than sufficient for 4K streaming, gaming, and large downloads. While it trails the speed leaders (NordVPN's NordLynx and ExpressVPN's Lightway) in head-to-head comparisons, the performance gap is rarely noticeable in everyday use. OpenVPN UDP is slower as expected but still adequate for most tasks.

## Streaming
Surfshark reliably unblocks Netflix, Amazon Prime Video, and Disney+ across multiple regions. BBC iPlayer access is consistent from UK servers. Hulu and Max can be less reliable, occasionally requiring server switching to find a working connection. The NoBorders mode helps in restrictive network environments.

## Value Proposition
At $2.49/month on the two-year plan, Surfshark is exceptional value — especially considering the feature set includes CleanWeb ad blocking, MultiHop, unlimited device connections, and a 30-day money-back guarantee. For households with many devices or users who want a full feature set at a budget price, Surfshark is hard to beat.

## Verdict
Surfshark is the best value VPN on the market. The unlimited device policy alone makes it the most economical choice for families and multi-device users. While speeds and streaming reliability don't quite match the premium tier, the gap is narrowing with each update — and the price difference is substantial.
